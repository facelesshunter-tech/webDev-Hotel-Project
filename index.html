OUR STORY

A Century of
Unrivaled Elegance

Since 1924, the Aurelian Grand has stood as a testament to the highest ideals of hospitality. Born from a vision of creating a sanctuary where discerning travelers could experience the extraordinary, every detail has been curated to transcend expectation.

From our hand-laid marble floors to our bespoke butler service, each element of the Aurelian experience is a deliberate act of artistry — a promise that every moment spent within our walls will be remembered long after departure.

"The art of living, elevated."
